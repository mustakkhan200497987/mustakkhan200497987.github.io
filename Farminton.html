Farminton

About project:

Basically Farminton is a Agriculture App for farmers and community.It improve Efficiency of crop production,management and distribution using digital technology.It is developed for broadcast news and latest findings on crop management to farmers. Farminton introduce latest methods to reduce negative environmental impacts. It targets Farmers,Supermarkets and Machinery Providers.
